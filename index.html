How I Work

1
Define the business question
Write what a stakeholder would actually ask, before touching any data.
2
Explore and clean
SQL and Python to understand structure, gaps, and what the data can and cannot answer.
3
Investigate
The right method for the right question. Segmentation, trend analysis, or correlation depending on what the data needs.
4
Find the signal
Not just what happened, but what it means for a decision. In my Superstore analysis, this is where I isolated the Tables sub-category as a -$17.7K profit drain, driven by ~26% discounts concentrated in the East region.
5
Communicate the finding
Power BI dashboards built for non-technical stakeholders. The goal is a decision, not a chart.
